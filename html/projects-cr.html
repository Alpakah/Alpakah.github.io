Sabertooth | UCLA (2022)

Sabertooth is the name for my Beetleweight (3 lb) Combat Robot. For reference, combat robotics are a clash between bots similar to the TV series "Battlebots™". Where as the TV series fights at the heavyweight division listed at 250 lbs, the general combat robotics scene across the world has a number of weight classes.

This project was also my first exposure to so many different skills: manual machining, 3D printing, soldering, and harnessing.

Slap&Go | UCLA, 2022

Slap&Go is an Antwieght (1 lb) Combat Robot that I built for a 10 week long class at UCLA.

Below is a (rather fun) demonstration of Slap&Go's test run, where I replaced its aluminum weapons with rubber hands... so it can "Slap" then "Go". Sadly its fun design did not transalte to much success in the cage, as it lost in the first round of competition. The video for this is also shown below.
