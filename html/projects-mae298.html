MAE 298: Aerospace Structures | UCLA (2024)

This page will be completed when the class concludes in Spring 2024.

MAE 298: Aerospace Structures Seminar was taught by Professor Vinay Goyal in the Winter of 2024 as a part of the UCLA graduate program in Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering. In this class, we focus on stress, fatigue, and bonded/bolted joint analyses. The most important takeaway from this class is an exposure to Abaqus, a Finite Element Analysis (FEA) software. I was able to take flight loads and apply them to representations of tangible aircraft structures and determine whether they would meet flight requirements or even fail.
