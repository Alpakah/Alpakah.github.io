T-Shirts

At the UCLA Samueli Makerspace, I had access to both stickers and heat transfer vinyl, as well as a vinyl cutter and heat press machine. All of this gave me the freedom to explore creating vinyl designs to be used for t-shirts and stickers!

One of my favorite past-times that I've developed is to design and print custom t-shirts. It's not only a great creative outlet for me to indulge my artistic side and draw fun designs, but I love that I can turn it into a practical item that I can wear -- they end up becoming perfect gifts! Stickers

The vinyl designs aren't limited to just T-shirts, but also stickers. They're great for medium-large scale signage and fun little designs to slap onto anything and everything: reusable water bottles, binders, etc.

Other Vinyl Creations

My vinyl creations don't end at just T-shirts and stickers. I've made a few designs that have went on some different mediums, like masks and even bibs!
