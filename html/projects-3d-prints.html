Miscellanous 3D Prints

In my free time, I enjoy 3D printing both functional and recreational objects alike. This passion for 3D printing comes from my time working at the UCLA Samueli Makerspace, where I have thousands of print hours creating prints for UCLA students to use for their classwork, club projects, and personal use and hundreds of more hours maintenancing and fixing the printers that have been running 24/7 nonstop for over 3 years. Giving myself these smaller projects continues to improve my proficiency in CAD, provides me with even more practice with 3D printing, and most importantly keeping me excited to continue creating in my free time and have fun with engineering.
