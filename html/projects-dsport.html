DSport Magazine | Fountain Valley, CA

DSport Magazine is a company that writes articles on automobile performance, and during my time with them from June to September of 2022, I got to work with the manufacturing team at this summer internship to machine car engines to improve efficiency as well as develop new skills with manual and CNC machining alike.

The

One of the operations used by the company is to increase the cross sectional area of two different cylinder head channels in an engine: those that lead from the intake manifold and those that exit to the exhaust manifold. To do this, I used a CNC probe and scanning tools to map out the exact outline of a manifold gasket. This outline then sets the path for the CNC router, for which the conversational G-Code cut within 0.0005" precision.
